About Us

Callie van der Merwe

Managing Director Attorney

After graduating from North-West University, Callie started serving his articles at a family law firm in Pretoria. He initially gained most of his experience in the domestic violence court and children's Court. His dream was to become a formidable criminal defense attorney, and he took an opportunity to work in a law firm where he worked with labour disputes, criminal defense, and deceased estates.


Since being admitted as an Attorney Callie kept on learning and advancing his knowledge and skills in criminal defense and deceased estates. He is passionate about his work and helping his clients.
